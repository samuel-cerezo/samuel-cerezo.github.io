RGBD Inertial Flow

My actual research topic

Goal

The goal of odometry is to estimate the system state from a set of visual, inertial, or other measurements. The state x consist of velocities, biases and gravity vector at different moments of time. We consider visual odometry (VO) and visual-inertial odometry (VIO), but the theory described here is general and can be applied to other sensors (GPS, LIDAR). We consider a VIO problem in which we want to track the state of the sensing system.
