    My Actual Research

    The objective of my doctoral thesis is to address the localization and construction of maps in highly dynamic and deformable environments. As a fundamental novelty of the proposed research, my main objective is to jointly formulate the location of a mobile RGB-D sensor and the estimation of a map of the scene, understanding as a map the estimation of the position of static objects, the movement of dynamic objects and deformation of deformable objects.
