Mi history

I was born in a city called San Juan, in the province of San Juan, Argentina. when I was one year old, I went to live in Rincon de los Sauces, a small town in the province of Neuquen, with my 3 siblings and my parents.

In 2007 I moved with my family to the capital of the province, with the intention of attending high school with an electronics orientation. So it was that in 2013 I obtained the title of electronic technician. The following year I started my electronic engineering career at the Universidad Nacional del Comahue. In 2020 and in the middle of the pandemic, I finished my undergraduate studies with the final project Compressive sensing system for photovoltaic panels.

In the year 2021 I won the place to start the PhD in systems engineering and computer science at the Universidad de Zaragoza, so I moved to the city of Zaragoza, Spain. I am currently in the second year of my doctorate.
