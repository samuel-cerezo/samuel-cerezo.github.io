Samuel Cerezo1, 1Universidad de Zaragoza

Dataset Structure

Every folder is structured as follows:

                       ├── rgb/
                       │   ├── 1739371345.939813614.png
                       │   ├── ...
                       ├── depth/
                       │   ├── 1739371345.939813614.png
                       │   ├── ...
                       ├── robot_data/
                       │   ├── joint_positions.txt
                       │   ├── flange_poses.txt
                       ├── associations.txt
                       ├── imu.txt
                       ├── groundtruth_raw.csv
                       ├── groundtruth.txt

File Descriptions

  • rgb/: Folder with color images (PNG, 8-bit per channel).
  • depth/: Folder with depth images (PNG, 16-bit, scaled by 1000).
  • associations.txt: Temporal associations of RGB and depth images.
  • imu.txt: IMU data (accelerometer and gyroscope readings).
  • groundtruth.txt: Ground truth trajectory in world coordinates.
  • extrinsics.yaml: Calibration parameters defining spatial relationships.
  • intrinsics.yaml: Internal parameters of the camera.
  • robot_data/: Joint positions and flange poses.
